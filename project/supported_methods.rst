Supported Methods
=================

OpenKinetics Predictor wraps published enzyme kinetics models behind one interface.

Prediction targets
------------------

- kcat
- Km
- kcat/Km

Supported engines
-----------------

- KinForm-H, kcat and Km.
- KinForm-L, kcat.
- UniKP, kcat and Km.
- DLKcat, kcat.
- TurNup, kcat.
- EITLEM, kcat and Km.
- CataPro, kcat, Km, and kcat/Km.
- CatPred, kcat and Km.
- OmniESI, kcat and Km.
- RealKcat, kcat and Km.
- IECata, kcat/Km.
- MMISA-KM, Km.

Model selection
---------------

Use several methods when you need comparison. Use one method when you need a single reproducible workflow.
